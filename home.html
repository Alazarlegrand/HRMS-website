NexaHR is a modern, role-based Human Resource Management System designed to streamline how organizations handle employees, attendance, payroll, and internal administration. It provides a structured workflow where Super Admins manage everything, Admins handle operations, and Users access controlled information in a secure environment.

Built to replace manual HR processes with a fast, centralized, and intelligent digital system.
